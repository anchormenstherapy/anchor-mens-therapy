Our story

Why I Started Anchor Men's Therapy.

About Jordan J. Caron →

I've sat across from men who've lost their savings, destroyed their credit, lied to their families, and felt trapped in a cycle they couldn't break. The shame and secrecy that accompanies gambling addiction often prevents men from seeking help until they've hit rock bottom.

What I've learned is that gambling addiction isn't about willpower or character — it's a mental health condition driven by underlying patterns of stress, avoidance, and dysregulated dopamine responses. Like other compulsive behaviors, it often serves as a way to escape difficult emotions or cope with unmet needs.

Today, gambling has become more accessible and more aggressively marketed than ever. With the rise of legalized online betting platforms, constant advertising, and betting integrated directly into sports broadcasts, many men find themselves caught in patterns they never intended to develop.

Through evidence-based treatment approaches like CBT, men can identify the triggers that drive their gambling, develop healthier coping mechanisms, and rebuild their financial and relational stability. Recovery is possible, and it starts with reaching out.

At Anchor Men's Therapy, we provide specialized, judgment-free support for men ready to break free from compulsive gambling and regain control of their lives.

Because you don't have to face this alone.

Jordan J. Caron
